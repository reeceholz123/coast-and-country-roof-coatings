How It Works

Four steps. No surprises.

No tricks, no upsells halfway through. Here's exactly how every Coast & Country job runs.

01

We climb up and measure.

Every quote starts on the roof. Proper measurements, tile condition checked, ridge caps and flashing inspected, any repairs flagged upfront. We tell you exactly what your roof needs — no guesswork.

02

You get a fixed written quote.

One written price, fully itemised, honoured for thirty days. No verbals. No "we'll work it out on the day". You know what you're paying before we touch a thing.

03

We prep properly, then coat.

Pressure clean, broken tiles replaced, ridge caps rebedded and repointed, rusted screws swapped, primer where it's needed. Then two coats of Dulux AcraTex Roof Membrane, masked around solar, gutters, walls.

04

You pay when you're happy.

No deposit required. We invoice on completion. You inspect the work before we leave the site. Warranty certificate in your hand the day we finish.

Common Questions

What homeowners ask.

What does roof restoration cost? +
Colorbond respray starts from $18 per square metre. Tile restoration sits a bit higher because of the prep involved. Every roof is different though — so we climb up, measure properly, and write you a fixed price. No rough estimates, no surprises. Got a lower written quote from another licensed restorer? We'll beat it by 20%.
How soon can you start? +
Typically within seven days of you accepting the quote, weather permitting. Bigger jobs or busy seasons (peak summer, pre-Christmas) can run a bit longer, but we'll give you a firm start date before you commit, not after.
Do I need to pay a deposit? +
No deposit. Ever. You pay when the job's finished and you're happy with it. Not a day before.
Can you paint the gutters and fascia at the same time? +
Yes — and most clients have us do both at once. We'll reseal, prime, and colour-match the gutters and fascia to your newly restored roof (or to your existing trim colour if you'd rather keep it). Easier to do it all in one visit than book a separate trade later.
What's included in the ten-year guarantee? +
Every job is backed by a ten-year written warranty on the Dulux AcraTex coating. If the paint peels, flakes, cracks, or fades in that window, we come back and sort it. You get the warranty certificate on the day we finish. Separate from that, we stand behind our workmanship too — if something's not right with how it was done, we fix it.
Can you work around solar panels? +
Yes. In most cases we carefully mask around the panels so the job gets done with them in place. If the panels need to come off for a clean finish, we'll flag it upfront in the quote and help coordinate with your solar installer.
