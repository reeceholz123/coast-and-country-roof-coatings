Roof Restoration
Raymond Terrace.

Locally based · Hunter River corridor · Port Stephens LGA

Coast and Country covers Raymond Terrace and the surrounding Hunter. We've been restoring roofs across this part of the world for years. Colorbond resprays, tile restorations, ridge capping repointing, the lot. On the ground, with a Dulux Accredited Applicator badge to back the work.

What Raymond Terrace roofs need.

Raymond Terrace homes catch a particular mix of weather: humid air drifting up the Hunter River, salt blown in from Port Stephens, and the strong UV of the lower Hunter summer. That combo wears coatings out faster than most people realise. Tile roofs grow lichen on the south sides; Colorbond chalks and fades; and the screws on older sheet roofs start to back out and rust at the heads.

For Raymond Terrace, we typically recommend Dulux AcraTex on Colorbond and a full clean / re-bed / re-point / coat cycle on concrete tile. Both come with our ten year written guarantee.

What we do here.

Across Raymond Terrace and the surrounding suburbs we handle the full restoration suite. Colorbond respray, tile restoration, screw replacement at $1.50 per screw, soft wash and moss treatment, heat reflective coatings, and commercial / shed roofs. Got something that's not on the list? Give Bryce a call, we probably do it.
